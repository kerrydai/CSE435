Lane Management System

Project Background

More and more modern cars are adding safety features. With the usage of cameras detecting the lane lines, the system can now assist drivers in driving.

Project Description

The Lane Management System is an automated driver assistance system designed to detect lanes and assist or warn drivers to stay within the lane.
